## How It Works

### Current — Step descriptions (~40 words each)
**Step 1 – Photograph:**
Using the iPhone's clinical-grade camera, your physician captures a high-resolution image of any lesion in under 10 seconds — no additional hardware, no workflow disruption, right at the point of care.

**Step 2 – Analyze:**
skinmap's AI model — validated on over 50,000 clinical images — instantly classifies the lesion and outputs a structured risk score with supporting diagnostic indicators while the patient is still in the room.

**Step 3 – Track:**
Every scan is stored in the patient's longitudinal record. At every future visit, skinmap automatically surfaces the previous scan for side-by-side comparison — turning each appointment into a data point in a complete skin health history.

## Key Features

### Current — Feature descriptions (~50 words each)
**Longitudinal Skin Tracking:**
skinmap stores every scan in a patient's permanent record, enabling side-by-side comparison across any timespan. Detect subtle morphological changes that a single-appointment visual exam would miss — and document them with clinical-grade precision.

**AI-Powered Risk Stratification:**
Our clinical AI model classifies lesions by risk level — from benign to high-suspicion — in under 3 seconds. Validated to 90% sensitivity and 90% specificity in internal testing across 50,000+ annotated clinical images representing diverse skin tones and lesion morphologies.

**Patient Management Dashboard:**
Manage your entire patient panel from a single, clean iPhone interface. Search by patient, filter by risk level, and review complete scan histories in the same time it takes to open a chart — without switching systems or apps.

**HIPAA-Compliant by Design:**
Every image, patient record, and AI output is stored with end-to-end AES-256 encryption and handled in full compliance with HIPAA regulations. skinmap is built for clinical environments where patient privacy is not a feature — it is a requirement.
